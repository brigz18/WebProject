Happy Valentine's Day Joy,

A calm soul, like morning dew,
With a heart that shines, bright and true.
Her mind is loud, though she's quiet too,
A gentle soul, with a fierce mind anew.
Her kindness spreads, contagious and bright,
Intelligence that intimidates, a beautiful sight.
She shines so bold, yet seeks no fame,
A quiet strength, with a heart that's not tamed.
